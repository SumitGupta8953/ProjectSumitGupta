Admission will be granted to the appicant in the first year of the diploma,undergraduate and Postgraduate courses on
ment/qualifying examination, And also on the basis of intervieq/ counseling.

  • PROCEDURE :-

    • For admission to various courses application on proscribed from to be made Counsellors shall assist or guide at the time of

  • SELECTION PROCEDURED :-

    • candidates who has passed the qualifying examination mentioned shall be eligible for admission to various courses. Admission

How To Reach SICS

Chitrakoot By Air


The nearest airport is Allahabad(135 kms), khajuraho(175 kms) and Varanasi(275 kms) from
Chitrakoot. Daily flights are available from these airport to Delhi.


Chitrakoot By Train


The nearest railway station is at Chitrakoot Dham Karwi(CKTD) which is 10 km distance from
Chitrakoot. Chitrakoot Dham station lies on the Jhansi-Manikpur main line and is well
connected to all major cities in India through Jhansi junction.(Railway Station Code: CKTD).
Trains to Manikpur Jn (Manikpur Junction is 35 kms away from chitrakoot. Manikpur Jn. lies on
on the Mumbai-Hawada main line via Allahabad.) Trains to Satna (Satna Junction is 80 kms away from Chitrakoot).

Chitrakoot By Road


State owned bus services connect Chitrakoot with Jhansi, Satna, Kanpur, Banda and Allahabad.
